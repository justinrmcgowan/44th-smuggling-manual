## Business Philosophy

This shopping list follows a tiered investment strategy:

**PREMIUM TIER (Haulers)** - These ships generate revenue and receive Grade A components across the board. Every aUEC spent here directly increases profit potential.

**STANDARD TIER (Primary Protection)** - The Vanguard and Scout receive quality Grade A/B components because they need to survive to protect the money-makers.

**BUDGET TIER (Expendable Escorts)** - The Cutlass and Gladius are designed to be sacrificed if needed. They get "good enough" Grade B/C components to keep costs down.

## Money-Saving Tips

If you need to reduce costs:

1. **Skip the 400i completely** - Save 443,900 aUEC (components) + 84,000 aUEC (weapons) = 527,900 aUEC
2. **Downgrade Vanguard to Grade B** - Save ~150,000 aUEC
3. **Buy fewer FPS loadouts initially** - Save ~200,000 aUEC (buy as needed)
4. **Reduce missile stocks** - Save ~100,000 aUEC (restock after first profitable runs)

**Minimum Viable Fleet:** C2 + MSR + Titan + Vanguard = ~2.8M aUEC
